Panda Birth Celebrated

A newborn panda was welcomed at our sanctuary, bringing hope for the species. We are thrilled to announce the birth of this adorable panda cub on October 10, 2023.

This new addition is a significant milestone in our ongoing efforts to protect and conserve these magnificent creatures. The cub and its mother are both healthy and doing well.

We extend our gratitude to our dedicated team and supporters whose contributions have made this celebration possible. Your support continues to drive our mission to safeguard pandas and their natural habitats.

Stay tuned for more updates as we closely monitor the progress and growth of this young panda cub.
